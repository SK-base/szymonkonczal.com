Building a personal website is a great way to showcase your work and share your thoughts.

## Why Build One?

- **Own your content**: No algorithm deciding who sees your work
- **Creative freedom**: Design it exactly how you want
- **Learning opportunity**: Great way to practice web development

## Getting Started

Start simple. You don't need all the features at once. Focus on content first, then add features as needed.
